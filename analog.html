Analog Week 2 Ruleset

Smartphone

  • Smartphone stays on silent mode at all times (no sound, no vibration).
  • No X, Reddit, TikTok, Instagram, news or similar. This applies also to the computer.
  • Put phone in grayscale mode.
  • No browser
  • Only allowed apps:
    • Snapchat (only for communication, no stories etc.)
    • City bikes, calendar, workout apps, banking apps, music/podcast apps
    • Only use maps, when there is some place that is really hard to navigate to with memorized instructions.
  • Notifications disabled for all apps except, still keep phone on silent, but these are visible when checking phone:
    • Snapchat
    • Calendar
    • Slack, during work.
  • Don't take the phone when going to sleep.

Nokia Phone

  • Acts as primary reachable device (non-silent).
  • Carried when out and about.

Video Content

  • Only allowed on the TV
  • Applies to YouTube, Netflix, TV series, or any video streaming.
  • Watch only with intention â€” decide what to watch before opening the app. When that is over, close the app.
  • Limit to around 1 hour, except for sports events.

Audio

  • Try to be mindful of filling out all downtime with audio.
  • Don't constantly change what to listen.

Notebook

  • Carry daily. Use as a wallet.
  • Prioritize taking notes and using this over using phone.

Polaroid

  • Take one instant photo per day.

Book

  • Always have a book with you that you can read during downtime or instead of picking up your phone.

Daily Reflection (Notebook)

At the end of each day, reflect briefly on the following for the experiment:

  • Did today feel busy or calm?
  • Did you feel creative and focused?
  • Approximately how much of your day was filled with video content?
  • Did you work out?
  • Did you work on own programming projects?
